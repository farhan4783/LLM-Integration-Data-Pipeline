Gemini is a family of multimodal large language models developed by Google DeepMind, serving as the successor to LaMDA and PaLM 2. Comprising Gemini Ultra, Gemini Pro, Gemini Flash, and Gemini Nano, it was announced on December 6, 2023, positioned as a competitor to OpenAI's GPT-4.
It powers the Gemini chatbot (formerly Bard). 

Sundar Pichai, CEO of Google, emphasized that Gemini was built to be multimodally natively, meaning it was trained on text, images, audio, and video interchangeably from the beginning.
